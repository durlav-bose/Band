This is Mister Misery

MISTER MISERY, spawned from the hollowed parts of Sweden in February 2018, is a modern horror metal band consisting of 4 unalive creatures, with the will to spark a flame in your restless souls! By blending high energy riffs combined with anthemic choruses, their sound borders the genres of both old school rock n roll, sleaze and aggressive modern metal, all mixed delightfully together to create the unique sound that characterizes MISTER MISERY today.

After the pace-setting release of their praised debut album “UNALIVE” in 2019, the band has not slowed down, despite the fact a global pandemic crushed schedules, MISTER MISERY kept busy during quarantine, writing new breathtaking material, while still releasing numerous compilations for their fans in the meantime.

MISTER MISERY,just launched their pre-order for their second full-length album “A BRIGHTER SIDE OF DEATH”, set to be released in April 2021. The first single of the album: “BALLAD OF THE HEADLESS HORSEMAN” is released combined with a mind-blowing music video on December 4th, which opens up the next chapter of their horror story! Stronger, scarier, and more diverse than ever.

The band will march through Europe on their 2nd headline tour soon while booking more shows for the year with the ambition that the world will be back on its feet and ready to go wild again!
